# About Me

I'm a Senior DevOps Engineer and AWS Specialist with deep expertise in cloud infrastructure, infrastructure as code, and technical leadership. I help organizations build scalable, secure, and cost-effective AWS environments.

## Background

With years of hands-on experience across AWS services, DevOps practices, and infrastructure automation, I've worked with teams of all sizes—from startups to enterprises—to design and implement cloud solutions that drive real business value.

My focus is on:
- **Modern Infrastructure as Code**: Terraform, OpenTofu, Terragrunt, AWS CDK, and CloudFormation
- **AWS Architecture**: Multi-account strategies, security, cost optimization, and well-architected reviews
- **DevOps Transformation**: CI/CD pipelines, automation, and observability
- **Technical Leadership**: Mentoring teams and guiding architectural decisions

## What I Believe

Great infrastructure is invisible—it just works. I'm passionate about building systems that are reliable, maintainable, and cost-effective. I believe in automation, documentation, and continuous improvement.
