# AWS for Startups

I've helped many startups launch successfully in the cloud. Getting your cloud foundation right from day one is critical — the choices you make early on determine how fast you can move, how manageable your operations stay, and how your costs scale as you grow.

## What I Bring to the Table

- **Move fast, stay in control**: I help you pick the right AWS services and architecture patterns so you can ship quickly without accumulating technical debt or runaway costs.
- **Cost-conscious from the start**: Startups can't afford to waste money on over-provisioned infrastructure. I make sure you're only paying for what you actually need, with a clear path to scale when the time comes.
- **Operational simplicity**: Complex setups slow teams down. I design infrastructure that's easy to understand, maintain, and hand off — so your small team isn't drowning in ops work.
- **Audit-ready**: Whether it's SOC 2, ISO 27001, or investor due diligence — I've helped startups pass audits successfully by building security and compliance into the foundation, not bolting it on later.

## Typical Engagements

- Setting up a well-structured AWS account and organization from scratch
- Designing CI/CD pipelines that let you deploy with confidence
- Infrastructure as Code so your environments are reproducible and version-controlled
- Cost optimization reviews to keep your burn rate in check
- Preparing for security audits and compliance certifications
